## Risk Mitigation

### Critical Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|-----------|
| Webhook delivery at scale | Medium | High | Use Bull queue, early load testing |
| Custom field migrations | Medium | High | Dry-run, automated rollback, backup |
| Multi-tenant isolation | Low | Critical | Security audit, pentest, row-level security |
| Scope creep | High | Medium | Weekly prioritization, hard cut-off |
| Performance at scale | Medium | Medium | 10x load testing, aggressive caching |
